RaspDuino Expresso Machine and Logging

In this project we aimed to explore what was possible when it comes to creating the perfect shot of expresso. We created a monitor and logging system for Peter's Arduino/RaspberryPi based Expresso machine. Sensors used - Pressure of the shot, Temperature of the shot, Ambient Temperature and Humidity Sensors.
